Guest Guide

You'll receive three keys: a gray key fob for the main entrance and lift, a larger key for the apartment door (turn counterclockwise to open), and a smaller key that locks your private room door.

Air Conditioning & Comfort

The apartment has air conditioning, plus your own Dyson fan in the room for extra airflow whenever you want it.

Pool, Jacuzzi & BBQ

You have full access to the outdoor pool, jacuzzi, and BBQ, including deck chairs and seating. Ask us if you'd like a beach towel.

Safety & Security

Use your key fob for building and lift access, and make sure doors close behind you. Your room has its own lock, and the ensuite bathroom's outer door has a latch you can push down for extra privacy.

Room Amenities

Your room comes with a modern Dyson fan for airflow on demand, a kettle with coffee and tea, your own fridge, a desk with a proper office chair, and a cassette blockout blind so you can sleep in even with the sun up.

Cooking isn't included in this stay, but you're welcome to the filtered cold water dispenser, cups, and utensils in the kitchen.

Kitchen & Dining

Your fridge space: the top shelf is yours to use. Need more room? There's space in the freezer drawer too — just let us know if you're struggling to fit everything in.

Using the kitchen: you're welcome to the cooktop (induction), oven (induction), microwave, air fryer, kettle, toaster, and sandwich maker. Ask us for a quick rundown on the induction cooktop and oven if you haven't used one before — if you want to use the oven the conventional way, the induction plate needs to come out first.

Leave dirty dishes in the sink and we'll put them away, or load the dishwasher yourself. Avoid metal utensils on the Teflon cookware.

The smoke detectors are very sensitive — when cooking anything smoky or steamy, pull the rangehood fully open and crack the balcony door for ventilation.

Tap water is safe to drink, plus there's a filtered water dispenser in the fridge door and an ice compartment in the freezer.

Laundry

The washing machine is just outside your room, opposite the door — an LG TwinWash, with a full-size washer up top and a small pedestal washer built into the base for quick, small loads.

  • Use the lower TwinWash pedestal to wash small loads faster.
  • Use the top washer for normal-sized loads.
  • Turn the machine on from the switch in the top right corner.
  • Use the round control panel to choose which washer (top or bottom), then pick your program.
  • Detergent is dispensed automatically — no need to add your own.

You're welcome to use the dryer too — choose a 45–60 minute cycle and clean the lint filter after. An iron and ironing board are available, and there's a drying rack on the balcony if you'd rather air-dry. Staying longer? Just ask if you need fresh linen or towels.
